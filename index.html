About Us

Grooming with Love,
Care & Expertise

At EJ Pet Styles, we believe every pet deserves to look great and feel comfortable. Our grooming services are designed to provide a safe, hygienic, and stress-free experience for your furry companions.

Located in Palavakkam, we offer personalized grooming services for different breeds and coat types, handled with patience and care.
